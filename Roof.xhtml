Rooftop Terrace

The gorgeous rooftop terrace offers not only the hot Morrocan sun to soak up, but also a beautiful view of the iconic Marrakchi skyline. You can see the smoke from the huge variety of food stalls in Jemaa el Fna, and even smell the food they make!

The terrace is the perfect place to sunbathe, offering complete privacy from neighbours whilst still allowing you to absorb the typical atmosphere in the Medina. If the sun gets too hot then the jacuzzi is the perfect place to cool down, and with the typical daytime temperature you will be dry in seconds! In the evenings the covered room provides the perfect place to socialise, read a book, or just escape from the dazzling sunshine for a cool break.

The terrace comes furnished with:

  1. 2 sunloungers
  2. Bathroom
  3. 2 dining tables
  4. 4 chairs
  5. Parasol
  6. Fully furnished covered room
