First Floor

On the first floor the Africa and Estelle bedrooms can be found, both boasting double beds and ensuite bathrooms. Even though the rooms remain cool during the hot summer months, the air conditioning can provide you with a comfortable temperature to sleep in. With a balcony looking over the ground floor, you can feel intimate with your fellow travellers, and yet still preserve your privacy relaxing in the large bedrooms. There is also a study which can double up as another bedroom.
