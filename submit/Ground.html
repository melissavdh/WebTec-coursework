Ground Floor

The ground floor is beautifully lit by the dazzling sun during the day while still remaining a cool temperature. There is a gorgeous fountain which adds to the feeling of complete tranquility, and four little tortoises which love to be fed! Plenty of plants helps the courtyard to be a lovely place to relax, with doors to the kitchen, sitting room, dining room, and downstairs bedroom.
